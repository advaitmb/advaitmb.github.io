\sectionTitle{Research Interests}{}
\vspace{3mm}

My research lies at the intersection of HCI, NLP, psychology, and computational social science. I study psychological safety in human-AI interaction and build user modeling methods from large-scale behavioral data. More broadly, I am interested in how AI systems shape beliefs, communication and relationships.
